The Bebop Crew

Spike, Jet, Faye, Ed, and Ein together on their adventures.

Spike Spiegel is the laid-back bounty hunter with a mysterious past. Skilled in martial arts and sharpshooting.

Jet Black is a former ISSP officer and the fatherly figure of the Bebop crew. Skilled pilot and mechanic.

Faye Valentine is the cunning and glamorous bounty hunter with a hidden past. Loves gambling and adventure.

Ed is the eccentric hacker prodigy, full of energy and quirkiness. Brilliant at computers and exploration. While ei is a brillant dog who ran away from a lab and ended up with the bebop crew.
