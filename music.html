Cowboy Bebop OST 1: Tank!

Composer: Yoko Kanno & The Seatbelts | Released: 1998

The first official soundtrack for Cowboy Bebop, blending bebop jazz, blues, funk, and swing into a soundscape as bold as the series itself. “Tank!” remains one of the most recognized anime openings in history — energetic, stylish, and timeless.
